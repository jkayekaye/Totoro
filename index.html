Totoro

Totoro is a magical forest spirit from My Neighbor Totoro (1988), a Studio Ghibli film by Hayao Miyazaki. Friendly and fluffy, he befriends two sisters, Satsuki and Mei, bringing wonder and adventure to their lives. Totoro has become an iconic symbol of childhood and Japanese animation.

My Neighbor Totoro

My Neighbor Totoro (1988) is a heartwarming Japanese animated film by Hayao Miyazaki and Studio Ghibli. It follows sisters Satsuki and Mei as they move to the countryside and discover Totoro, a gentle forest spirit. With beautiful animation and a nostalgic, magical atmosphere, the film explores themes of childhood, family, and nature. It is considered a classic of Japanese animation.
